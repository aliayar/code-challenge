        Description

        Hieronymus Bosch was a Dutch/Netherlandish painter from Brabant. He is one of the most notable representatives of the Early Netherlandish painting school. His work, generally oil on oak wood, mainly contains fantastic illustrations of religious concepts and narratives. Wikipedia
        Born: 's-Hertogenbosch, Netherlands
        Died: August 9, 1516, 's-Hertogenbosch, Netherlands
        On view: Museum Boijmans Van Beuningen, MORE
        Periods: Northern Renaissance, Renaissance, MORE
        Nationality: Dutch
        Influenced: Pieter Bruegel the Elder, Salvador Dalí, Joan Miró, MORE
        Series: Visions of the Hereafter Polyptych, Hell and the Flood
